
You run through maze, collect loot and fight monsters.

Controls:
  Movement:
    [W] - Move up
    [S] - Move down
    [A] - Move left
    [D] - Move right
  
  Interactions:
    [E] - Pick up item
    [F] - Equip item from inventory
  
  Overlays (toggles):
    [?] - Show this guide
    [i] - Show info about current tile
